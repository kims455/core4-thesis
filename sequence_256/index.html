Memories in Dots

Statement

I often ask myself: Where does memory begin? How do you continue the journey? Where does it lead in the end?

This website presents my brain as an algorithmic symbol which is an interactive network. Two hundred fifty-six images represent my neurons, organized into six themes (Coincidence, Abstract, Micro/Macro, Me–Family–Friends, Pure Joy, Muse/Inspiration). They float and flow across the page, as if inside my thoughts. Some briefly swell and shrink, passing through my mind like fragments of memory. The lines represent the connection between memories. I collected data for all the photos to show that each one has its own special identity and context.

This is my way of showing you my brain. How would you show yours?
